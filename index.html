About the Doctor

Dr. Banka Soujanya is a compassionate and experienced Gynecologist and Obstetrician with over 13 years in medicine, including 4 years as a specialist. She currently practices at Rohitha Specialty Clinic, Pragathi Nagar, Hyderabad.

She completed her MBBS from Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences in 2012, followed by DGO from Kaloji Narayana Rao University of Health Sciences in 2019.
